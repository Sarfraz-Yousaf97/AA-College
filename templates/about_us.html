AA Computer College is one of the most prestigious computer education institutes in City that supports the educational and career progression needs of more than 20,000 students every year. The Institute aspires to be a center of excellence in Computer Sciences and technologies and acts as an effective agent of change and a model for others to emulate. The institute has been catering to the needs of aspiring and experienced IT Professionals. We offer a wide range of short-term and long-term customized Training Courses suitable for small, medium and large-sized organizations. The courses start from basic Computer literacy to advanced levels in Programming, Networking, Hardware, Multimedia resources and its applications. Well-equipped computer labs are also available to the students for drop-in use when classes are not in session.

Our Vission

With its focus on students’ success, “AA Computer College” prides itself on its high quality offered programs being a trademark for more than two decades, offering a variety of courses preparing students for advanced studies and challenging positions in the government and business industry. The Institute aspires for the leadership role in the pursuit of excellence in Programming, Networking, Hardware, Multimedia resources and its applications.

Our Mission

AA Computer College believes that Information Technology has a part to play for people from all walks of life.Our mission is to make technology an asset and provide cutting-edge IT knowledge. We want to remain a reliable name in computer education by anticipating and satisfying the ever-growing demands of the IT industry.

Carear Opportunities

For nearly 2 decades, AA Computer College has been dedicated to redefining higher IT education and empowering our students with the skills they need to excel in the classrooms and throughout their professional sphere keeping a career-oriented approach. Seeking to equip our students with essential management competencies, coupled with an appreciation of professional accountability and social responsibility, the institute has developed programs that prepare professionals for meaningful careers.
