Accomodation

AA Computer College also provides the facility of hostel for its Students. Hostel accommodation is available for the male. Accommodation is allotted on a first-come, first served basis. Furnished rooms are available in a double storied building. Each hostel block offers a friendly and secure environment for its inhabitants and is equipped with a wide range of facilities including laundry, kitchen, TV lounges, and easy access to dining facilities. Dedicated hostel staff is accessible to students for help at all times, and emergency services including transport facilities are available.

Security and Safety

In addition to highly effective Campus security arrangements, all hostels have protective boundary walls, security Cameras and dedicated staff at each block.

Hostel Facilities

In order to facilitate the students, the following facilities have been provided at both the boys and girls hostels; Mess, Mosque, Separate Television room, Telephone, Newspapers, Guest Rooms, common Room, Computer Room, Electric Water Coolers, High-speed internet and Wifi, Hostel Laundry and 24 hours security.
