Mission

AA Computer College provides a complete, appropriate, excellence and globally recognized IT-based education at all levels and disciplines with the objective of creating professionals and leaders.

Vission

The Vision of AA Computer College is to provide the essential training to technical professionals and Provides universally standard for the support of technology and of technical professionals in educating global conditions.

Aim
